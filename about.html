About me


UI/UX Designer with experience in Color & Material Design and Development

I started my career as a textile designer, collaborating with local hosiery factory in Samobor, Croatia. In 2014 I started working as color & material designer at Puma HQ in Herzogenaurach. I worked with Key Accounts on SMU orders, designing color cards and selecting key drops for various markets. In 2016 I switched to Adidas to do color design for Training Footwear and Slides. As of 2017 I am a colorist for C&A HQ in Düsseldorf in charge for color management and communication. From there I switched my interest to digital design area and completed education in UI design, I am working on UI/UX projects focused on visual design for web and mobile.
